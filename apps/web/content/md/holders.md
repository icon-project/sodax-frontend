# SODA Holders

**Buy SODA today. Burning on 18 networks.** SODA is built to be scarce — capped supply, deflationary by design, live across 18 networks.

## How SODA grows the protocol

- **Every swap burns SODA.** Partners across 18 networks collect fees, which are used to buy back and reduce SODA supply.
- **Supply capped at 1.5B.** No emissions, zero inflation guaranteed.
- **A stronger DAO governs ever-growing liquidity.** Protocol revenue funds DAO growth, drives trade, and rewards stakers.
