# SODA Exchange

**Trade, stake, and earn across networks. Cross-network DeFi, all in one place.** The SODA Exchange is the official SODAX user interface for swap, save, borrow, stake, pool, and migrate operations across 18+ supported blockchain networks. All operations route through the SODAX solver and intent system.

## Apps

- **Swap** — cross-network token swaps via the SODAX solver and intent system. Trade between assets that live on different chains in a single transaction. https://sodax.com/exchange/swap
- **Save** — supply assets to the SODAX money market and earn yield. https://sodax.com/exchange/save
- **Borrow** — borrow against supplied collateral. Multi-network collateral and borrow flows. https://sodax.com/exchange/loans
- **Stake** — stake SODA for xSODA and earn 20% of protocol revenue. Higher APR for longer locks (up to 180 days). https://sodax.com/exchange/stake
- **Pool** — provide AMM liquidity and earn from swap fees. https://sodax.com/exchange/pool
- **Migrate** — migrate self-custody ICX 1:1 to SODA. https://sodax.com/exchange/migrate

## How it works

SODAX is intent-based: users express what they want (e.g. "swap 1 ETH on Ethereum for USDC on Solana") and a solver fulfills the intent atomically. SODAX coordinates execution, liquidity, and failure recovery across heterogeneous networks so users never see bridges or intermediate steps.

- Hub chain: Sonic
- Supported networks: 18+ (Ethereum, Base, BNB Chain, Polygon, Optimism, Arbitrum, Avalanche, Kaia, ICON, Injective, Nibiru, Solana, Sonic, Stellar, Sui, HyperEVM, Bitcoin, Near, Stacks, Redbelly, LightLink)
- Solver throughput: 10 intents/second, 30k quote requests/second, $10k swap capacity (with $50k coming soon)
