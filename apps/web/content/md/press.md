# SODAX Press

The SODAX press hub lives at **https://press.sodax.com** — that's where the full press kit, announcements, brand assets, and media resources are maintained. Visiting `https://sodax.com/press` redirects there.

## What's at press.sodax.com

- Latest SODAX press releases and announcements
- Brand assets (logos, color palette, typography, usage guidelines)
- Media kit (boilerplate copy, founder bios, photography)
- Media contact

## Other SODAX-managed resources

- News articles and product updates: https://sodax.com/news
- News RSS feed: https://sodax.com/news/feed.xml
- Brand assets archive (logos + token): https://sodax.com/SODAX.logos.and.token.zip
- Marketing MCP (brand, glossary, voice & guardrails — machine-readable): https://marketing.sodax.com/mcp
