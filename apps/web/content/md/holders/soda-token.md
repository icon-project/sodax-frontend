# SODA Token

## What is SODA?

SODA is the native utility and governance token of the SODAX execution coordination system. SODAX is built as an outcome-oriented cross-network execution system that coordinates liquidity and execution across heterogeneous networks.

## Token economics

- **Total supply: 1.5 billion** — fixed. No more can be minted.
- **Deflationary by design** — 20% of protocol revenue is used to buy SODA from the market and burn it permanently, linking supply reduction to system usage.
- **Vesting** — locked SODA follows a 5-year vesting schedule with a 1-year cliff. Long-term alignment, gradual distribution.
- **Protocol-Owned Liquidity (POL)** — protocol-managed liquidity that supports execution reliability and solver inventory across networks.

## Staking

Stake SODA for **xSODA** to receive 20% of protocol revenue. Higher APR for longer locks, up to 180 days. Early unstaking is allowed but incurs a penalty, which is redistributed to stakers who remain committed.

## Networks

SODA is live on **18+ networks**, including Sonic, Ethereum, Arbitrum, Avalanche, Solana, Sui, Stellar, and more.
