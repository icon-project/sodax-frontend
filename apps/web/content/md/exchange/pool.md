# SODAX Pools

**SODAX AMM pools and liquidity provision.** Provide liquidity to SODAX pools to earn a share of swap fees generated through the AMM.

## What it does

Liquidity providers deposit pairs of supported assets into AMM pools. Pool LP positions are NFT-based. LPs earn a share of trading fees proportional to their liquidity contribution.

## Key facts

- **NFT-based LP positions** — concentrated liquidity with on-chain position tracking.
- **OHLCV candle data** available for every pool via the public Builders MCP.
- **Cross-network swaps route through pools** alongside solver-supplied liquidity.
- **Deflationary tie-in** — fees collected by pools partly fund the SODA buy-and-burn.
