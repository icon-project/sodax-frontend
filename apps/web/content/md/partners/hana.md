# Hana Wallet — case study

**Multi-Network Wallet & Payments App.** Hana Wallet integrated the SODAX SDK to enable cross-network trades in roughly 22 seconds across 14+ networks. Users trade and spend crypto without ever thinking about bridges or networks.

## The fit

For multi-network wallets that want to give users a single unified experience for swaps, payments, and asset movement across heterogeneous chains — without requiring users to understand which network an asset lives on.

## Outcomes

- Cross-network trade settlement in **~22 seconds**.
- **14+ networks** accessible from a single wallet UX.
- Users stop managing bridges and asset locations themselves.
