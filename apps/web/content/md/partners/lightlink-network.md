# LightLink Network — case study

**Ethereum Layer 2.** LightLink integrated SODAX at the network layer to give applications on LightLink access to cross-network liquidity and execution across 14+ networks. Users trade assets like BTC, SOL, and AVAX without bridging delays.

## The fit

For new and emerging networks (L2s, app-specific chains, alt-L1s) that want applications building on them to launch with day-one access to cross-network capital and assets — without each application having to integrate bridges itself.

## Outcomes

- Network-layer integration: applications on LightLink inherit cross-network capabilities automatically.
- **14+ networks** of liquidity available to LightLink-deployed apps.
- Cross-network swaps for assets like **BTC, SOL, AVAX** without traditional bridge wait times.
